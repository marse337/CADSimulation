Customer Lookup and Retention Tool

This page helps identify a customer as a MONI or a Livewatch customer, and whether the account should be transferred to retention. You may search by address, phone, or email. Searching by address requires at minimum a house number in Address Line 1 and a valid zip code to work, and does not search Livewatch's data.
